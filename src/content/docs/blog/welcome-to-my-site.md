Welcome to my little corner of the internet. This site is my personal homepage — a place where I can share what I'm working on, what I've learned, and what I'm thinking about.

## What you'll find here

This site is organized into a few sections:

- **About** — who I am and what I do
- **Blog** — where I write about things I find interesting
- **Projects** — things I've built or contributed to
- **Now** — what I'm focused on currently

## Why build this?

I wanted a space on the web that's entirely my own. No algorithm, no feed, no noise — just content I choose to publish, when I want to publish it.

## Tech stack

This site is built with [Astro](https://astro.build) and [Starlight](https://starlight.astro.build), a documentation theme that works just as well for personal sites. The content is written in Markdown, and everything is statically generated into plain HTML.

It's simple, fast, and intentionally low-fuss.
